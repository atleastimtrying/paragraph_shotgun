Paragraph shotgun 2

Paragraph shotgun is an experimental bookmarklet, its aim is to speed up the reading of documents by removing paragraphs with a simple click.

There are some limits to what you can kill:

  • If you kill a link you're still directed to the page it links from.
  • If try to kill text outside of one of the supported tags it just won't die.
  • If you're on a page based on tables havoc can be unleashed as a <td> could contain alot more than a few words or numbers.
  • You need to have javascript enabled otherwise you've left your ammo at home.

The shotgun is below simply drag and drop it into your bookmarks bar

The shotgun is currently designed to kill smaller elements rather than big game like articles, sections, headers, footers, uls and so on. The elements below are vulnerable:

  • <p> a paragraph
  • <li> a list item
  • <h1><h2><h3><h4><h5> 5 different sizes of headings
  • <span> a selection of text
  • <img> images
  • <td><th> table cells

I originally wrote this project in jquery, injecting extra libraries and all kinds of shinnanegans. It's now written in minified vanilla js and is super quick and easy to understand!
